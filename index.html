// FAQ

COMMON
QUESTIONS

Straight answers to questions before we work together.

How long does a project take?+
Most websites ship in 2–4 weeks. AI projects range from 1–8 weeks depending on complexity. Realistic estimate after discovery call.
Do you work globally?+
Absolutely. I work with clients worldwide. Async communication via Slack or email, flexible with timezones for calls.
What does pricing look like?+
Typically 50% upfront, 50% on delivery. For longer projects, milestone-based payments available. All terms in contract before we start.
Can you take over existing projects?+
Yes. I work with inherited codebases regularly. I audit first and give honest assessment of what to keep and what to rebuild.
Do you offer maintenance?+
Yes. Monthly retainer packages for ongoing development, updates, monitoring, and support. Custom options available.
What AI tools do you use?+
OpenAI API, Anthropic Claude, LangChain, LlamaIndex for orchestration. For automation: n8n, Make, custom Python pipelines.
